201020_eventVsMonthly.xlsx
Raw and processed amount-weighted, event-based precipitation isotope data for two monitoring sites; used for comparison with monthly integrated samples.

cluster.csv
Cluster group membership for monitoring stations, based on analysis of Tulley-Cordova et al. [2018].

NNData.rda
Full Navajo Nation water isotope dataset, generated through a query to the Waterisotopes Database and saved in lines 4-9 of the script analysis.R.

precipAmt.csv
Monthly preciptation amount data from Tulley-Cordova et al. [2018].

tucson.csv
Published precipitation isotope data from Tucson, AZ, acquired from https://www.geo.arizona.edu/node/154 on 12/18/2020.
